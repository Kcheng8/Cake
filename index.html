Our Story

Handcrafted Excellence Since 2018

Velvet Crumbs began with a passion for creating beautiful, delicious cakes that bring people together. Founded in the heart of Brisbane, we've built our reputation on quality, creativity, and genuine care for every celebration we're part of.

Each cake is made to order using premium ingredients sourced from trusted local and international suppliers. We don't believe in shortcuts—every layer, every decoration, every flavour is thoughtfully crafted.

Whether it's a milestone birthday, a dream wedding, a baby announcement, or a corporate event, we'd love to be part of your special moment. We serve Brisbane and surrounding suburbs with pride.
